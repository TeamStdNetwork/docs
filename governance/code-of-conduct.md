# Code of Conduct

This Code of Conduct outlines expectations
for participation across the Std ecosystem.

## Our Standards

Participants are expected to:
- Be respectful and considerate
- Provide constructive feedback
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Act in good faith within the community

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks or abusive language
- Trolling or disruptive behavior
- Any form of exploitation or harm

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible
for enforcing this Code of Conduct.

Violations may result in warnings,
content removal, or participation restrictions.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all
repositories, discussions, and interactions
within the Std ecosystem.
