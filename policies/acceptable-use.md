# Acceptable Use Policy

This policy defines acceptable and unacceptable use
of repositories and resources within the Std ecosystem.

## Acceptable Use

Projects may be used for:
- Learning and educational purposes
- Building tools, bots, and automation systems
- Research and experimentation within legal boundaries
- Open-source development and contribution

## Prohibited Use

Projects must not be used for:
- Illegal or malicious activities
- Privacy violations or data abuse
- Harassment, exploitation, or harm
- Circumventing security mechanisms
- Violating platform terms or applicable laws

## Responsibility

Users are solely responsible for how they use
the tools and resources provided.

Maintainers are not responsible for misuse,
illegal actions, or damages caused by third-party use.
