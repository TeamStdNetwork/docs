# Privacy Policy

This document explains how information is handled
across projects within the Std ecosystem.

## Information Collection

The Std ecosystem does not intentionally collect
personal user data through its repositories.

Some projects may interact with third-party services.
Any data handling performed by those services
is governed by their respective privacy policies.

## Logs and Technical Data

Certain services may generate technical logs
for debugging, security, or performance monitoring.

These logs may include non-personal technical details
such as timestamps, request metadata, or error traces.

## Data Usage

Any collected technical data is used strictly for:
- Improving reliability
- Preventing abuse
- Debugging and maintenance

No data is sold, shared, or used for advertising.

## Responsibility

Users are responsible for understanding
the privacy practices of third-party services
they choose to integrate with.

The maintainers are not responsible for
external services or platforms.
